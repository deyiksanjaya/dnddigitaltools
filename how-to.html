Getting Started

Welcome to your digital D&D tool, designed for seamless gameplay. This app saves everything directly in your browser, so there's no need for an account. If this is your first time, you'll be prompted to create a character. This will open the Settings modal where you can fill in your hero's details. For an app-like experience, you can also add this page to your phone's homescreen.

The Main Dashboard

The main screen gives you immediate access to your most-used stats and tools.

  • Character Header: At a glance, see your character's name, level, race, class, and current/max HP. The health bar provides a quick visual cue of your status.
  • HP Management: Use the input field below the health bar to quickly apply healing or damage. Enter a positive number (e.g., 10) to heal or a negative number (e.g., -10) to take damage. Press the Apply button or hit Enter to confirm.
  • Dice Display: This central area shows the result of your most recent roll with a cinematic animation.
  • Dice Grid: All standard D&D dice (d4 to d100) are ready for a quick click.

Rolling the Dice

Rolling is designed to be fast and intuitive:

  • Simple Rolls (d4-d12, d100): Just click the die you want to roll. The result appears instantly.
  • Smart Rolls (d20): Clicking the d20 opens a "smart roll" modal. Here, you can select a Saving Throw or a Skill. The app automatically fetches the correct modifier from your character sheet and adds it to the roll, saving you time and mental math. You can also choose to roll a plain d20 without any modifiers.
  • Auto-Copy: If enabled in settings, the complete roll result (e.g., "d20 (Stealth) Roll: 18 (Dice: 15, Modifier: +3) | HP: 20/20") is automatically copied to your clipboard, perfect for pasting into a chat or virtual tabletop.

Character Sheet Details

All your character's information is organized and automatically calculated for you.

Spells & Features

This section lists your cantrips, spells, and class features. For leveled spells, it tracks remaining slots and provides a Use button to expend a slot. For features and cantrips, a Log Use button is available to announce the action without consuming resources.

Wealth & Wisdom

  • Currency: The currency section is interactive. Simply type in the fields for any coin type (PP, GP, EP, SP, CP). Press Enter or click away to save the new total.
  • Adventure Notes: This is a free-form text area for all your campaign journaling. Your notes are automatically saved when you click away from the text box.

More Character Info

This collapsible section contains the core of your character sheet, including:

  • Core Combat Stats: Armor Class, Initiative, Speed, and your Proficiency Bonus.
  • Ability Scores: Your six core abilities (STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, CHA) and their calculated modifiers.
  • Saving Throws & Skills: A complete list where final modifiers are automatically calculated based on your ability scores and proficiencies. Proficient skills are highlighted for clarity.

Health & Death's Door

The app includes a fully automated Death Saving Throw system to manage those critical moments.

  • Automatic Trigger: When your HP drops to 0, the Death Save modal immediately appears.
  • Making Saves: Click the button to make a Death Saving Throw (a d20 roll). Successes and failures are tracked automatically.
  • Outcomes: The system handles all results: a Natural 20 revives you with 1 HP; 3 successes make you stable; and 3 failures result in character death.
  • After Death: If the worst happens, you are given the option to Restart Same Character (restoring them to full health) or Start New Character (resetting the app).
  • Auto-Summary: When the death save sequence concludes, a summary of all rolls and the final outcome is copied to your clipboard (if auto-copy is enabled).

Settings & Data Management

The Settings button gives you full control over your character and the app.

  • Form Editor: Edit every aspect of your character, from their name and level to their ability scores, skill proficiencies, and spell slots.
  • Spells & Features Editor: Add or edit spells and features using a simple format in the text area: Name / Level / Description. Use 0 for cantrips, 1-9 for spell levels, and feature for class features.
  • JSON Editor: For power users, clicking "Edit with JSON" reveals the raw data for your character. You can copy this text to back it up, share it with a friend, or import a new character by pasting their data in and hitting Save.
  • AI Prompt Template: A dedicated button to copy a detailed prompt for playing solo D&D with an AI Dungeon Master (like ChatGPT).

Solo D&D with an AI Dungeon Master

Want to play D&D but don't have a group? Use this tool in conjunction with an AI Dungeon Master. The app provides a pre-built, detailed prompt to get you started.

  1. Click the Settings button at the bottom of the main page.
  2. Inside the settings modal, click the "Copy AI Prompt Template" button.
  3. Paste the copied prompt into your favorite AI chatbot (e.g., ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude).
  4. The AI will now act as your Dungeon Master! Use this app to manage your character and roll dice as you play through your solo adventure.
