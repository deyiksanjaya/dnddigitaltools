Getting Started

Welcome to your digital D&D tool, designed for seamless gameplay. This app saves everything directly in your browser, so there's no need for an account. If this is your first time, you'll be prompted to create a character. This will open the Settings modal where you can fill in your hero's details. Let's dive into the features.

The Main Dashboard

The main screen gives you immediate access to your most-used stats.

  • Character Header: At a glance, see your character's name, level, race, class, and current/max HP. The health bar provides a quick visual cue of your status.
  • HP Management: Use the input field below the health bar to quickly apply healing or damage. Enter a positive number (e.g., 10) to heal or a negative number (e.g., -10) to take damage. Press the Apply button or hit Enter to confirm.
  • Dice Display: This central area shows the result of your most recent roll with a cinematic animation.

Rolling the Dice

The dice grid contains all standard D&D dice. Rolling is simple:

  • Simple Rolls (d4-d12, d100): Just click the die you want to roll. The result appears instantly in the display area.
  • Smart Rolls (d20): Clicking the d20 opens a "smart roll" modal. Here, you can select a Saving Throw or a Skill. The app automatically fetches the correct modifier from your character sheet and adds it to the roll, saving you time and mental math. You can also choose to roll a plain d20 without any modifiers.
  • Auto-Copy: If enabled in settings, the complete roll result (e.g., "d20 (Stealth) Roll: 18 (Dice: 15, Modifier: +3) | HP: 20/20") is automatically copied to your clipboard, perfect for pasting into a chat or virtual tabletop.

Wealth & Wisdom

Managing your inventory and story notes is crucial for any adventurer.

  • Currency: The currency section is interactive. Simply click on the number for any coin type (PP, GP, EP, SP, CP) to edit it directly. Press Enter or click away to save the new total. It's fast, simple, and requires no extra buttons.
  • Adventure Notes: This is a free-form text area for all your campaign journaling. Jot down NPC names, quest details, or suspicious-looking statues. Your notes are automatically saved when you click away from the text box.

Diving Deeper

The "More Character Info" section is a collapsible dropdown that contains the heart of your character sheet. Here you'll find:

  • Core Combat Stats: Armor Class, Initiative, Speed, and your Proficiency Bonus.
  • Ability Scores: Your six core abilities (STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, CHA) and their calculated modifiers.
  • Saving Throws & Skills: A complete list of all saving throws and skills. The final modifier for each is automatically calculated based on your ability scores and proficiencies.
  • Spells & Features: A list of your known spells, class features, and racial traits.

Health & Death's Door

The app includes a fully automated Death Saving Throw system.

  • Triggering Death Saves: When your HP drops to 0, the Death Save modal automatically appears.
  • Making Saves: Click the button to make a Death Saving Throw (a d20 roll). The result determines your fate: 20 revives you with 1 HP, 10-19 is one success, 2-9 is one failure, and a 1 is two failures.

Advanced Customization

The Settings button gives you full control. Here you can edit every aspect of your character, from their name and level to their ability scores and skill proficiencies.

For power users, clicking "Edit with JSON" reveals the raw data for your character. You can copy this text to back up your character, share it with a friend, or import a character someone else has shared with you by pasting their data in and hitting Save.

Add to Homescreen

Turn this web tool into a full-fledged app on your mobile device for offline use and quick access. Follow the steps for your device:

iOS (iPhone/iPad)

  1. Launch Safari on your device.
  2. Navigate to this page.
  3. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Scroll down and select "Add to Home Screen."
  5. Tap "Add" in the top-right corner to finish.

Android (Chrome)

  1. Launch Chrome on your Android device.
  2. Navigate to this page.
  3. Tap the three vertical dots in the top-right corner of the browser.
  4. Select "Add to Home screen."
  5. Follow the on-screen prompts and tap "Add."
